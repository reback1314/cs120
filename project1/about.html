About Us

Hailing from different cities, with different scars, but brought together by music, Vesper Noir's vocals, Elias Frost's cool arrangements, Zane Vex's wild guitars, and Ryder Blush's rhythmic drums have combined to create a soul-stirring musical storm. Their first show was in an abandoned station in Tokyo late at night, where thousands of people flocked to hear the awakening of ‘Night of Souls’. Now they are on a world tour, bringing their music to every sleepless soul in the world.

Elias Frost | Piano/Keyboard | 25 years old

Real Name: Shiro Suu

Birthplace: Sapporo, Hokkaido

Personality: Calm and collected, perfectionistic to the point of obsession, cold exterior but warm inside.


Story Snippet: Elias Frost, born Shiro Suu, is a pianist whose music is described as weaving poems of coldness and passion between the black and white keys. A musical prodigy since childhood, his experimental style, blending classical and electronic music, was deemed "blasphemy to classical music" at the Vienna Conservatory. Tragedy struck with the early death of his equally gifted programmer sister. Her last code became an integral part of his keyboard, generating melodies in her style with every performance, as if her soul lived on through music. Expelled from the conservatory after a rebellious performance, he wandered the world, collecting piano strings to build a massive metal harp for spirit's melodies. Meeting Vesper Noir and hearing her voice rekindled his belief in the power of music.

Ryder Blush | Drums | 20 years old

Real Name: Sakuraji

Birthplace: Kyoto

Personality: Lively and cheerful, creative, but with a hidden fear of failure.


Story Snippet: Ryder Blush, born Sakuraji, was once a gifted gymnast, even on the path to the Olympics. A severe competition error ended his gymnastics career, crushing his dreams. During recovery, he discovered percussion, finding rhythm and heartbeat in drumming. He abandoned his past to become a drummer. He trains with dumbbells to enhance his drumming power and practices in a soundproof gym-turned-studio. His performances blend gymnastics and music. He also anonymously runs an emotional radio station, helping people express themselves through drum rhythms.

Zane Vex | Guitar | 23 years old

Real Name: Honjou

Birthplace: Osaka

Personality: Wild and unrestrained, extremely confident, with the edge of a street punk.


Story Snippet: Zane Vex, born Honjou, is a guitarist whose guitar ignites notes in the whirlwind, letting the broken world hear the cry for freedom. Growing up in a motorcycle gang in Osaka, he was more devoted to motorcycle customization than guitar practice. His grandmother, a shrine maiden, gave him a talisman hoping for his life's purpose, which he found in the roar of engines and soul-shaking music. Discovered after creating captivating sounds by striking steel bars on a construction site, Vesper Noir recruited him. His stage antics include tearing antique shirts, symbolizing the collapse of the old world. He has 12 earrings in his left ear, each representing a city where he listened to street sounds and translated them into his guitar music.

Vesper Noir | Vocals | 22 years old

Real Name: Amatsuki Rin

Birthplace: Shinjuku, Tokyo

Personality: Cool and aloof, with a gentle heart, untamed yet highly responsible.


Story Snippet: Vesper Noir, born Amatsuki Rin, is a vocalist whose voice is described as a blade in the night, cutting through silence and awakening dormant souls. Orphaned and raised by a kind orphanage director, Rin inherited a silver chain with an ancient wolf totem, a memento and a cryptic guide for her life's journey. She started her musical journey in underground clubs, her husky and sharp voice expressing her loneliness and anger. Discovered by a music producer, she became a star, yet remains grounded, feeding stray cats in the back alleys of her record company and cherishing her silver harmonica—a link to her unknown jazz musician father, whom she continues to search for, much like her own voice.
